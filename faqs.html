07 — FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most from clients.

How do I place an order or book a service?⌄
Simply send us a message through Facebook, Instagram, or email. To help us prepare an accurate quotation, please include the following details if available:
  • 🎉 Type of celebration or project (Birthday, Wedding, Debut, Baptism, Business, etc.)
  • 📅 Event or preferred delivery date
  • 📍 Venue or location (if applicable)
  • 🛍 Service(s) you're interested in
  • 👥 Estimated number of guests or quantity needed
  • 💡 Your ideas, theme, colors, or inspiration (optional but helpful)
  • 💰 Budget range (optional, so we can recommend suitable options)

Don't worry if you don't have all the details yet — we'll gladly guide you through the planning process and help you find the best option for your celebration.

Do you accept customized requests?⌄
Yes! Personalization is one of ASC's specialties. Whether it's handcrafted keepsakes, event styling, printed materials, or gifts, we'd love to create something unique for your celebration.
How early should I book?⌄
We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance, especially during peak celebration seasons, to ensure availability and enough time for production.
Do you offer event setup services?⌄
Yes. Depending on your chosen package, we can provide event styling, backdrop setup, rentals, and on-site coordination.
Can you deliver nationwide?⌄
Availability depends on the product or service. Digital and printed items can often be shipped, while event services are currently available within our service areas.
How do payments work?⌄
A reservation fee may be required to confirm your booking. The remaining balance can be settled according to the agreed schedule. See our Policies page for full payment terms.
What occasions do you cater to?⌄
We proudly serve:
WeddingsDebutsBirthdays BaptismsCorporate EventsBusiness Branding School EventsSpecial Celebrations
