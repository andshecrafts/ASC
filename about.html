01 — About ASC

Crafting Moments Worth Remembering

And She Crafts (ASC) is a creative studio based in Pasig City that transforms meaningful moments into personalized experiences through handcrafted products, professional printing, event styling, rentals, and creative event solutions.

Our Story

How it began

And She Crafts began with a simple passion for creating personalized handcrafted pieces that brought smiles to people's faces. As we grew, we realized we weren't just making products — we were becoming part of our clients' most meaningful milestones.

From birthdays and graduations to weddings, business launches, and life's everyday celebrations, every order represents a story, a memory, and a moment worth celebrating.

Mission

To transform life's meaningful moments into unforgettable experiences through creativity, craftsmanship, and heartfelt service.

Vision

To become one of the Philippines' most trusted creative studios for personalized celebrations.

Brand Promise

We promise to make every celebration feel more meaningful through thoughtful creativity, personalized experiences, and dependable service.
