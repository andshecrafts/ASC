Booking Guide

Here's what to expect, from your first message to celebration day — and how we actually work along the way.

How Booking Works

From inquiry to celebration day.

01

Send Us Your Inquiry

Message us through Facebook, Instagram, or email to start the conversation.

02

Tell Us About Your Celebration

Share the details so we can prepare an accurate quotation.

  • Occasion
  • Event Date
  • Venue
  • Estimated Guest Count
  • Services Needed
  • Inspiration Photos
  • Budget (Optional)
03

Receive Your Personalized Quotation

We'll recommend the right services based on your vision, guest count, and budget.

04

Confirm Your Booking

A reservation fee secures your date and begins the planning process.

05

We Begin Planning & Crafting

Design, sourcing, and production happen here, with updates along the way.

06

Celebrate Your Special Day with ASC

We deliver, set up, and — if booked — stay on-site to coordinate.

This Is What It Feels Like to Work With Us

How We Bring Your Vision to Life

01

We Listen

Every celebration starts with your story. We take the time to understand the milestone, the people involved, and the feeling you want your guests to walk away with.

02

We Plan

We translate that vision into a clear plan — matching the right services, materials, and timeline to what your celebration actually needs.

03

We Design

From concept sketches to color palettes, we shape the look and feel of your event or keepsake before a single piece is made.

04

We Craft

This is where it comes to life — by hand, with care, whether it's a styled backdrop, a printed invitation, or a personalized keepsake.

05

We Celebrate Together

On the day itself, we're there to set up, coordinate, and make sure every detail reflects the moment you imagined.
