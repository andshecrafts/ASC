Booking Guide

Here's what to expect, from your first message to celebration day — and how we actually work along the way.

How Booking Works

From browsing to celebration day.

Booking with us has always started with a conversation — now there's an easier way to walk into that conversation prepared.

01

Browse Services

Explore the catalog at your own pace — photos, packages, and pricing for every category, whether you're planning a celebration or a business project.

02

Build My Quote

Add anything that catches your eye to My Quote as you browse — nothing here commits you to a thing. Think of it as thinking out loud, with photos.

03

Tell Us About Your Project

Everything in My Quote carries over automatically — just fill in a few more details so we can prepare an accurate proposal.

  • Occasion or Project Type
  • Event or Delivery Date
  • Venue or Location
  • Estimated Guest Count or Quantity
  • Inspiration Photos
  • Budget (Optional)
04

Receive Personalized Proposal

We'll refine what you've selected — or recommend the right services from scratch — based on your vision, quantities, and budget.

05

Reservation Fee

A reservation fee secures your date or slot in our production schedule.

06

Project Confirmation

Your booking is confirmed, and we begin planning and crafting — with updates along the way until it's time to celebrate.
